The Time Travelling Tech Titans

Time Machine Development

All these tech billionaires, who have seemingly unlimited resources, secretely developed a functional time machine in the early 21st century. This technology allows them to travel to different points in the past and future.

Influence Over World Leaders

These tech titans use their time machine to travel back in time and manipulate world Leaders, either by providing them with advanced knowledge or by directly influencing their decisions. This would explain unexpected political shifts and decisions that benefit these billionaires' interests.

Patent Theft and Innovation

These tech titans also use their time machine to steal ideas, patents, and innovations from history. They then claim these innovations as their own, thus accumulating wealth and power throughout time.

Control of Technology

These tech titans have been strategically suppressing certain technological advancements to maintain their monopoly on specific industries, preventing the public from accessing groundbreaking technologies ahead of their time.

Immortality Quest

The ultimate goal of these tech titans is to discover a way to achieve immortality by manipulating historical events, ensuring their influence spans generations.
