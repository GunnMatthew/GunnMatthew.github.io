The Time Loop Simulators

Ancient Builders

An ancient and highly advanced civilization, perhaps from another dimension or galaxy, created our universe as a complex, time-looping simulation.

Endless cycles

Time and history are not linear but are instead stuck in a continuous loop. The builders of the simulation manipulate events and time, resetting history at will.

Selective Memory

The memories of individuals are altered at the end of each loop, so people never realize that they are living in a repeating timeline.

Expiriments and Observations

The builders conduct various experiments within the simulation to observe human behavior and responses to different scenarios. They manipulate events, test potential outcomes, and monitor the results.

Mandela Effect

The "Mandela Effect" (collective false memories) is attributed to glitches in the simulation when changes are made to the historical narrative. These glitches are said to be a result of the builders' interventions.

Proof Concealment

Any evidence suggesting we're living in a simulation is actively suppressed or erased by the simulation's operators to prevent the truth from surfacing.

The Awakening

A subset of believers think that some individuals might break free from the simulation, gaining a glimpse into the real world, and that these "awakened" individuals might hold the key to understanding the true nature of our reality.
